What is it about?

SeedersBlock as a tool for building perpetually growing communities, which encourage their members to add value to the community through the built-in rewards structure and receive rewards in the form of Getseeds Tokens. Ultimately, for talented people in artworks and writings to showcase their talent to the world.

Social Network Reward System

Built using a cryptocurrency reward system. Receive some amounts of Getseeds into their Getseeds Wallets on the SeedersBlock.

Reapers (Organization)

Companies can hire users of SeedersBlock for digital jobs, like web development, graphics designs, using our decentralized Getseeds.

Inbuilt Market place

The Tokens can be exchanged for goods and services on the market place. The platform is easy to use and understand.

Advertisement Portals

Seeders can upgrade their account to advertise their product and services using Getseeds Token for payment.
